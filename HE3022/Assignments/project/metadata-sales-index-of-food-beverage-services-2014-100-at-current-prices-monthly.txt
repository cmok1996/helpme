Description:
  - 'The Food & Beverage Services Index (FSI) measures the short-term performance
    of food & beverage (F&B) services industries based on the sales records of F&B
    services establishments.'
  - ''
  - 'The FSI is presented at both current prices and constant prices. The indices
    at current prices measure the changes of sales values which can result from changes
    in both price and quantity. By removing the price effect, the indices at constant
    prices measure the changes in the volume of economic activity.'
  - ''
  - 'The base year is 2014 (2014=100).'
